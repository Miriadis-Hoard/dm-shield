Falling damage

1d6 bludgeoning damage for every 10ft3,0m fallen (max 20d6), lands prone, unless it avoids taking damage from the fall.

Suffocating

A creature can hold its breath for 1 + its CON modifier minutes (minimum 30s).

When out of breath or choking, it can survide for its CON modifier rounds (minimum 1). It can't be stabilized or regain hit points until it can breath again.

Food & Water

A character can go 3 + CON days (minimum 1) without food. After that, they suffer 1 level of exhaustion per day.

Drinking less than necessary requires a DC15 CON saving throw. On a fail, suffer 1 level of exhaustion.

Grapple

Premise. Use an Attack action to grapple a creature up to one size larger than you, within your reach.

Check. Athletics versus Athletics or Acrobatics.

Escaping. Target uses its action to roll the check again.

Dragging. You can move with the grappled creature at half speed, unless it's two or more sizes smaller than you.

Passing through

Overrun. Athletics versus Athletics to move through the target's space with an action or bonus action. Advantage if bigger than the target.

Tumble. Acrobatics versus Acrobatics to move through the target's space. Advantage if smaller than the target.

Shove

Straight. Athletics versus Athletics or Acrobatics to knock a target (up to one size larger than you) prone or push it 5ft away with an action.

Aside. Use shove with disadvantage to move the target to a space within reach.

Concentration

Casting. You lose concentration if you cast another spell that requires concentration.

Taking damage. DC10 or half the damage taken (choose the higher) to maintain concentration.
